PLY Mouse True Value Calculator

This estimates what PLY’s share price could look like if Mouse sells X units over a chosen time period. It converts units into net revenue to PLY, then applies either a profit multiple (P/E) or a revenue multiple. This is a scenario tool, not a prediction.
