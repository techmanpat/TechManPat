Review & Disclosure Policy

This page explains exactly how TechManPat handles reviews, sponsored content, and affiliate links. Transparency and viewer trust come first, always.

Contact

Our Commitment

TechManPat is built on honesty, independence, and real-world testing. This policy exists to make our standards clear to viewers, brands, and agencies.

We do not blur the line between reviews and advertising. We do not trade opinions for money. And we do not compromise editorial control.

Agreement to This Policy

By sending products to TechManPat or engaging us for any form of content, you agree to this policy in full. These terms are non-negotiable.

If these conditions do not align with your expectations, please do not send products or request coverage.

Product Reviews (Unpaid)

Product reviews on TechManPat are always unpaid and independent.

  • No payment. Reviews labeled as “Product Review” are never sponsored.
  • No editorial influence. Brands do not approve scripts, opinions, or final cuts.
  • No softening. Issues, bugs, and shortcomings are openly discussed.

If a product improves after review (for example via firmware updates), we may acknowledge that in follow-up content. Our goal is accuracy over time, not one-off impressions.

Our audience comes first. If a product underperforms, viewers will hear about it.

Editorial Videos (Sponsored Content)

Editorial videos are sponsored overviews, not reviews.

  • Clearly disclosed. Sponsored content is always labeled and compliant with platform rules.
  • No review score. Editorials showcase features and usage, not verdicts.
  • Editorial control retained. TechManPat controls the narrative and final edit.

Sponsors may be informed of issues prior to release, but sponsorship does not prevent honest discussion. If something materially affects the user experience, it will be mentioned.

We only accept sponsorships that align with our content and audience. Viewer trust always outweighs commercial opportunity.

Affiliate Disclosure

TechManPat uses affiliate links to help support the channel.

  • Affiliate links may appear in video descriptions or on this website.
  • Most links are Amazon Australia affiliates, with occasional brand-specific links.
  • Using these links does not increase the price you pay.

Affiliate commissions help cover production costs such as equipment, software, and time. They do not influence coverage, opinions, or conclusions.

Affiliate links are always disclosed clearly in accordance with platform and regulatory guidelines.

In Short

TechManPat exists to serve viewers, not brands.

  • Reviews are unpaid and independent
  • Sponsored content is clearly labeled
  • Affiliate links do not affect opinions
  • Editorial control always stays with TechManPat

This policy is our badge of honour and the foundation of trust with our audience.
